Computer & Laptop Service

No Signal Issue

If your monitor shows "No Signal," we check and reconnect cables, test the graphics card, and ensure the correct display input is selected. We'll also diagnose and repair any power or motherboard issues that could be causing the problem.

Hard Disk Not Showing

When your hard disk isn't detected, we inspect connections, recover data from corrupted drives, and ensure your BIOS settings are correct. If necessary, we replace the hard disk or help with data recovery.

Keyboard Not Working

If your keyboard stops responding, we check the connection, clean the keyboard, and troubleshoot software or driver issues. If the keyboard is damaged, we offer replacement services with compatible models.

Operating System (OS) Installation

We provide a fresh installation of your preferred operating system (Windows, macOS, Linux), ensuring all necessary drivers and software are set up correctly. We also back up your data before installation to prevent any loss.

Display Issues (Flickering, Dead Pixels)

For display issues like flickering or dead pixels, we check cables, test your graphics card, and reinstall drivers. If your screen is physically damaged, we offer replacement services to restore full functionality.

Overheating / Cooling Issues

When your computer overheats, we clean out dust, check fans, and reapply thermal paste to improve cooling efficiency. We also offer cooling upgrades and help you optimize ventilation for better performance.

Motherboard Issues

We diagnose motherboard problems like random shutdowns or errors, testing the power supply, cooling, and hardware connections. If needed, we repair or replace the motherboard and ensure your system is stable.

Virus or Malware Removal

We scan and remove viruses, malware, and unwanted software from your computer. After removal, we provide tips on safe browsing and recommend antivirus solutions to protect your system from future threats.

Computer Assembly Services

When providing custom PC building or assembling services, it's important to break down the offerings by the intended use case and performance level.

Basic Assembly Package

Ideal for everyday computing needs such as browsing, word processing, and streaming.

  • Processor: Intel Core i3 / AMD Ryzen 3
  • RAM:4GB DDR4
  • Storage: 500GB HDD or 240GB SSD
  • Graphics: Integrated Graphics (Intel UHD / AMD Vega)
  • Motherboard: Entry-Level with necessary I/O ports
  • Cooling: Basic air cooling
  • Usage:Email, web browsing, office tasks, light media consumption

Mid-Range Assembly Package

Perfect for multitaskers, students, and home office setups who need a reliable and fast system.

  • Processor: Intel Core i5 / AMD Ryzen 5
  • RAM:8 / 16 GB DDR4
  • Storage: 1TB HDD or 500GB SSD
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GTX 1650 / AMD RX 570
  • Motherboard: Mid-range with better connectivity
  • Cooling:Standard air cooling or aftermarket air cooler
  • Usage:Office work, multimedia, light gaming, video streaming, and content creation.

High-Performance Gaming & Workstation Package

Designed for gamers and professionals who demand high-end performance for gaming, video editing, or 3D rendering.

  • Processor: Intel Core i7 / AMD Ryzen 7 or Higher
  • RAM:32GB DDR4
  • Storage: 1TB SSD + 2TB HDD
  • Graphics: NVIDIA RTX 3060/3070 or AMD Radeon RX 6700 XT
  • Motherboard:High-end motherboard
  • Cooling:Advanced air cooling
  • Usage:High-end gaming, content creation, video editing, 3D modeling, heavy multitasking.

Custom Workstations for Professionals

(Custom Requirements) For specialized tasks like video production, CAD design, AI work, or large data processing.

  • Processor: Intel Xeon
  • RAM:32 + 32 GB DDR4
  • Storage: 2TB+ SSD with additional HDD storage
  • Graphics: NVIDIA Quadro or AMD Radeon Pro
  • Motherboard:High-end motherboard
  • Cooling:Advanced air cooling
  • Usage:Video production, 3D rendering, complex data modeling, CAD/CAM, scientific computing.
