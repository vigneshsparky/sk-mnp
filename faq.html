1. What services do you offer at your computer service center?

We offer a wide range of services, including:

  • Computer repairs and troubleshooting

  • Custom computer assembly

  • Printer repair and maintenance

  • Tally software support and troubleshooting

  • Virus and malware removal

2. How do I know if my computer needs repair?

If your computer is slow, freezing, showing error messages, or has hardware malfunctions (e.g., no power, broken screen, or damaged keyboard), it may need repair. Bring it to us for a diagnostic check.

3. Can you fix my printer if it's not working?

Yes! We provide repair services for all types of printers, including laser, inkjet, and dot matrix. Common issues we fix include paper jams, print quality problems, and connectivity issues.

4. Do you offer computer assembly services?

Yes, we can assemble custom computers according to your needs. Whether it's for gaming, office use, or other specific purposes, we help you choose the right components and assemble them into a fully working system.

5. How long does it take to repair my computer?

Repair time depends on the problem. Simple issues may be fixed in a few hours, while more complex problems could take 1-2 days. We will give you an estimated timeline once we diagnose the issue.

6. Can you recover data from my damaged hard drive?

Yes, we specialize in data recovery services. If your hard drive is damaged, we can often recover lost files such as documents, photos, and videos.

7. My computer is running very slow. Can you help?

Yes! Slow computers may need a tune-up, including cleaning up temporary files, upgrading hardware (like RAM or SSD), or removing malware. We can help make your computer faster.

8. What is Tally support?

Tally is an accounting software, and we offer installation, setup, troubleshooting, and support services for Tally users. If you are facing any issues with Tally, our experts can help you resolve them.

9. How can I prevent my computer from getting viruses?

To protect your computer from viruses, use an updated antivirus program, avoid opening suspicious emails or links, and be cautious when downloading files. We can also help you install antivirus software and perform security checks.

10. Do you provide services for laptops?

Yes, we offer repair services for both desktops and laptops. Whether it’s a screen replacement, battery issue, or software problem, we can help.

11. Can you help me set up my home or office network?

Yes, we provide networking services, including router setup, Wi-Fi troubleshooting, and network security configuration for both home and office environments.

12. I need to upgrade my computer. Can you help with that?

Absolutely! We can assist with upgrading your computer’s hardware, such as adding more RAM, installing a faster hard drive, or upgrading the graphics card to improve performance.

13. My printer is showing a "paper jam" error, but there’s no paper inside. What should I do?

This could be a sensor issue. We can check your printer and clean or repair any faulty sensors. Sometimes, paper dust or debris can trigger false paper jam errors.

14. How can I contact you for support?

You can contact us by phone, email, or visit our service center in Trichy. We also offer online support for software-related issues. Check our contact page for more details.

15. Do you offer warranty on repairs or services?

Yes, we offer a warranty on our repair services. If the same issue reoccurs within the warranty period, we'll fix it at no extra cost.
