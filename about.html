About Us

  We are a leading and rapidly growing computer service company based in Manapparai. Our journey began on April 4, 2016, at Palaniyandavar Tower, Manapparai, Trichy. Today, we are proud to have expanded with a branch office at Shangugam Complex, Viralimalai.

At our company, we are committed to delivering exceptional services to all types of customers, from individuals to businesses of all sizes. We also offer specialized support for startups, helping them thrive in the digital age.

Our products are known for their high quality, and we consistently receive positive reviews from our customers. Additionally, we are proud to be Udyog Aadhar registered and certified, further demonstrating our dedication to excellence.

Thank you for visiting our website and considering our services. We look forward to serving you and hope to welcome you back soon.

Our Story

At SK Computers, our journey began with a simple vision: to make technology accessible and reliable for everyone. The founders, tech enthusiasts with years of experience in the industry, recognized a growing need for a dedicated computer service provider that not only sells products but also prioritizes customer satisfaction. With this vision, SK Computers was born. Over the years, we have expanded our offerings, building a reputation for excellence and reliability. Whether you’re a student looking for an affordable laptop, a professional seeking high-performance hardware, or a business in need of comprehensive IT support, SK Computers is here to help.
